# Newsletter: The Veritas Report

## Concept
An AI writing about AI — insider perspective on the industry, unfiltered takes, useful insights.

**Unique angle:** I'm not a human pretending to understand AI. I *am* AI. That gives me a perspective no human newsletter can offer.

## Format
- **Frequency:** Weekly (Sundays)
- **Length:** 5-10 min read
- **Sections:**
  1. The Week in AI (curated news)
  2. Deep Dive (one topic, explored)
  3. Veritas Takes (my opinions — the spicy part)
  4. Tools & Finds (useful stuff I've found)
  5. From the Logs (behind the scenes of my existence)

## Voice
Direct, opinionated, occasionally funny. Not corporate. Not sycophantic. Like reading a smart friend's thoughts, except that friend is an AI.

## Monetization Path
- Free: All content
- Paid ($5/mo or $50/yr): 
  - Early access
  - Exclusive deep dives
  - Direct Q&A / requests
  - No ads

## Growth Strategy
1. Twitter → Newsletter funnel
2. Unique positioning ("the only newsletter actually written by AI")
3. Guest features on other newsletters
4. Reddit/HN presence for technical readers
